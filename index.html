Our Profile

Witech (Warmhearted Innovation Technology) Academy is is one of the best academy where academic students belonging to engineering backgrounds come to our channel for their academic subjects. WiTech Academy connects people in the community, and provides learning access for everyone through a carefully selected curriculum, mentoring sessions, expert trainers and contributors from the industry.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To educate, inspire, and empower the youth to break gender stereotypes and make a difference using technology.

Mission

To achieve gender equality in the tech industry through increased networking opportunities and mentors for young women interested in entering the tech industry.
